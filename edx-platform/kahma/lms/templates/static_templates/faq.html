${_("REGISTRATION")}

CREATE YOUR ACCOUNT

Before signing up for courses, you need to create your own user account. Click on Create Account in the navigation bar above to get started. You will be redirected to the registration page. Fill in your details and accept the Terms and Conditions to proceed.

ACTIVATION LINK

Once you have created an account, you MUST click the activation link that was sent to your e-mail address. Check your spam folder if you did not receive the activation link in your inbox. Once you have clicked the activation link, your account is active.

CUSTOMISING YOUR PROFILE

Once your account is active, you will be able to customise your profile by adding a profile photo, your preferred language, and even a short description about yourself. This information will be visible to your fellow learners and the KahmaVet course team. To get to your profile, click the dropdown menu next to your name on the navigation bar above (you must be signed in) and then click Profile. There are also important Account settings available in the dropdown menu.

${_("COURSE SIGN UP")}

HOW DO I SIGN UP FOR A COURSE?

To view all available courses, click on Courses in the navigation bar above. To learn more about each course, click on it to be navigated to the Course About Page, which offers more details about the course. If you want to be signed up for that course, please request access by contacting KahmaVet.

WHERE IS MY COURSE?

Once KahmaVet has granted you access to the course, the course will show up on your profile. Simply click on Dashboard on the navigation bar above (you must be signed in) to see all the courses you have been signed up for.

START LEARNING

In your dashboard, click View Course on the course you want to view. Once redirected, you will be inside the course, and you will see tabs such as Home, Course, Progress and Discussion. The Home tab shows messages from the course team. The Course tab shows all the course content such as videos and tests. The Progress tab will start showing your test results as soon as you have attempted some tests. The Discussion tab shows the discussion forum and usually some FAQs.
